PRESIDENT'S MESSAGE


President's Message

JUBILEE SCHOOL has the support and backing of a prominent socio-welfare organisation which is in existence for the last Six decades and spread its wings in this part of Bangalore by sheer dedicated hard work.

JUBILEE SCHOOL earnestly strive to enable students to succeed in their life offering a wide variety of challenging, enjoyable and successful curricular opportunities, athletic programmes, performing arts and musical programmes.

It is our belief that the school will be a unique experience for every student. Our school experience sets the stage for success later in life and provides a lifetime of positive memories for our students.

We are proud of our goal of adopting a holistic method for providing an excellent education for our students. We have a dynamic, hardworking faculty who are involved in education to benefit the students. Every member of our teaching faculty is well trained and equipped with the latest gadgets to impart quality education to our students.

Our school is also proud of its commitment to technology with digital Smart class facility and computers to help students and staff. We are constantly striving to stay on the cutting edge of technology with Software programme.

Academic excellence with character and personality development is our ultimate goal. The well chalked out academic and co-curricular programme enables the students to attain their full potential to develop sound work habits. The academic work is focussed on highly personalized attention and supervision. It is needless to mention that Education is a shared commitment between dedicated teachers, motivated students and enthusiastic parents with high expectations. We pledge to give back every parent the worth of their hard earned money and realise dream of their children.
