ADMISSIONS


Duly filled applications forms shall be submitted to the School Office along with photocopies of birth/caste/transfer certificates on or before the date notified in the school notice board.

  • Admission to Jubilee School is normally to the LKG classes.
  • Students can also be admitted in 1st to 9th standard based on vacancies.
  • The Applications Forms can be obtained from the school office from the date notified every year.
  • Admission for VIII standard will be opened from the day after the VII standard result.
  • Admission for VIII standard will be opened from the day after the VII standard result.
  • The decision of the Principal/Management is final with regard to admission.
